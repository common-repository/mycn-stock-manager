MyCN Stock manager is a plugin to connect between your store and MyCN Warehouse

== Description ==

Inventory management is a vital part of e-commerce operations, and you need to closely track your online store orders
to ensure that you can manage your inventory in an efficient manner that maximizes your business's cash flow while meeting
customer demands as quickly as possible.

So MyCN Stock manager is a plugin to connect between your store and MyCN Warehouse.
